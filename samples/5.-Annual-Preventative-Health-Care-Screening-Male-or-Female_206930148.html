DrCloudEHR : 5. Annual Preventative Health Care Screening Male or Female

Created by Pankaj Bhagat on Aug 19, 2024

The following outlines the process for completing this screening.

  1. Access the patient's chart and select "annual preventative health." Choose "new" to initiate the screening process and specify whether it is for a male or female.
  2. Input the applicable data into the system. If there are any female-specific conditions relevant to the patient, select those options.
  3. The report generated will include contact information for the patient and the date of completion. Choose today's date for this entry.
  4. If there are no issues to report, indicate that accordingly. Continue filling out the necessary information in the form.
  5. For completion data, provide details as required. This includes documenting any depression screenings, interviews, observations, or dementia screening if applicable.
  6. Enter the patient's blood pressure reading in the designated field.
  7. If liver disease testing was performed, select that option if it applies to the patient.
  8. Save the completed information. You may have multiple records for the annual preventative healthcare screening.
  9. To generate a report, select the relevant year and click on "generate data." This will create a preventative screening report based on the entered information.
  10. The report can be printed or retained in the patient's chart as needed.

This process ensures that all relevant health information is captured and documented effectively during the annual preventative health screening.
