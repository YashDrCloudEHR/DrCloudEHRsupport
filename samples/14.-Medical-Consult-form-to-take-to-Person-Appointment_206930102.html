Hereâ€™s how to print it using the calendar view:

  1. Open the calendar view to see your scheduled appointments.
  2. Identify the appointment you need, such as J. Jones' initial cardiology appointment.
  3. Click on the appointment to view more details.
  4. Navigate to the "related" section and scroll down to find the medical consultation form.
  5. Select the form to open it.
  6. Fill out the reason for the visit with as much detail as possible, as this information is crucial for the physician.
  7. Print the completed form.
  8. Have the escorting staff initial and sign the form.
  9. Take the printed form to the appointment.
  10. After the appointment, the doctor will fill out the next section of the form and may return it via fax or other means.
  11. Once you receive the completed form, go to the person's chart in the system.
  12. Upload the returned file to the "files" section.
