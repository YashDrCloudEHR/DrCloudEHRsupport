DrCloudEHR : Create an Encounter Form

Created by Tejasvini Aurangabadkar, last modified on Jan 07, 2019

To begin an encounter, click the patient's name in the appointment entry. The Patient Summary page for the patient opens, as well the Past Encounters and Documents page, which displays previous encounters or files that were uploaded for the patient. New patients may have no encounters listed.

To view details about the patient, such as transactions, patient history, or billing information, click the links at the top of the page.

To edit any information, click the Edit button and make changes as needed.
