DrCloudEHR : EnSoftek Support Team Ticket Definitions

Created by Ben Henig, last modified by Chris Goodbaudy on Sep 13, 2022

The DrCloudEHR Support Team uses multiple ticket statuses to inform the Client of the progress of support tickets.


The definitions are as follows:


Open: EnSoftek has received the ticket and has not yet reviewed the request.

In Progress/Fix in Progress: EnSoftek has reviewed the ticket and is currently working on its resolution.

DrCloudEHR Investigating:Â EnSoftek has received the ticket and is reviewing the request and may reach out with questions.

CR in Progress: A change Request is being created so the Customer can approve. No work is started unless approval is received.

Reporter Verification: A solution has been provided and requires the Customer to verify that the solution is acceptable; once approved, the solution will be deployed to production as necessary, and the ticket will be closed.

Customer Action/Waiting for Customer: This means Customer input may be needed. Either a response to comment on the ticket for further clarification or verification of a solution that is provided.

Deploy the Solution: The solution is deployed to the production system

Solution Provided: Development Team has provided a solution for the ticket, waiting for the Client to review and comment

Closed: All work on the ticket has been completed and verified as complete.
