DrCloudEHR : 5. How to Assign a Person to a Bed

Created by Pankaj Bhagat, last modified on Aug 19, 2024

Assigning a person to a bed can be accomplished through the Person's tab in the system. Follow these instructions to ensure a smooth check-in.

  1. From the Persons tab, select the person's name you wish to assign to a room.
  2. Scroll down to the "Admit Discharge" section and select "New Room Booking."
  3. Enter the check-in date for the booking.
  4. The system will automatically populate the person's name and date of birth, confirming the details for checking them into the selected bed on the specified date.
  5. Choose the appropriate room type. In this case(as shown in video), a shared male bed is available.
  6. Once you select the shared male bed, the booking will be created.
  7. You can verify the assignment by checking the person's chart, where you will see the room number assigned (e.g., 8720 24).
  8. By following these steps, you can efficiently assign a person to a room and ensure that all necessary information is recorded accurately.
