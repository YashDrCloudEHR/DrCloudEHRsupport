DrCloudEHR : 10. Editing a Diagnosis in Patient Charts

Created by Pankaj Bhagat on Aug 19, 2024

To edit or assign an end date to a diagnosis in a patient's chart, follow these instructions:

  1. Navigate to the patient's chart and locate the section labeled "Services and Diagnosis."
  2. Look for the drop-down arrows on the side of the diagnosis list.
  3. If the patient no longer has a specific diagnosis, such as a sleep disorder, click on the drop-down arrow next to that diagnosis.
  4. Select the "edit" option from the drop-down menu.
  5. Enter the end date for the diagnosis to indicate that it is no longer active.
  6. After entering the end date, ensure to save the changes.

Once saved, the diagnosis page will reflect the new information, showing that the diagnosis is no longer active with the specified end date.
