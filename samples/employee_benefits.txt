Employee Benefits Package

Health Insurance
The company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage including medical, dental, and vision plans. Coverage begins on the first day of employment. Employees can choose between a PPO plan or an HMO plan. The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 60% for dependents.

401(k) Retirement Plan
We offer a 401(k) retirement plan with company matching. The company matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary. Employees are eligible to participate after 90 days of employment. Vesting occurs over 4 years with 25% vesting each year.

Paid Time Off
Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year, 10 paid sick days, and 8 paid holidays. Vacation days increase to 20 days after 3 years of service and 25 days after 5 years. Unused sick days can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days.

Professional Development
The company provides an annual budget of $2,000 per employee for professional development including conferences, courses, certifications, and training programs. Employees can also request time off for professional development activities.

Wellness Program
We offer a comprehensive wellness program including gym membership reimbursement up to $50 per month, annual health screenings, mental health support through our EAP program, and quarterly wellness workshops.
