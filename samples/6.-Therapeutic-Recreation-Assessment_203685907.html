DrCloudEHR : 6. Therapeutic Recreation Assessment

Created by Pankaj Bhagat, last modified on Aug 06, 2024

The Therapeutic Recreation Assessment is a form used to evaluate an individual's recreational needs and interests as part of their therapeutic care. This assessment includes fields for:

  • Description and Historical Background: Provides context and history related to the individual's recreational activities.
  • Recreational Involvement: Documents the individual's participation in recreational activities.
  • Data Evaluation and Review: Based on documented evidence, this section evaluates the data collected during the assessment.
  • Goals and Progress: Sets specific recreational goals for the individual and tracks their progress over time.

The assessment aims to develop a personalized plan that enhances the individual's quality of life through recreational activities.

To use the Therapeutic Recreation Assessment Form, follow these steps:

  1. Log In and Select Person:
    • Log in to the system.
    • Select the person receiving services.
  2. Navigate to Forms Section:
    • Navigate to the forms section.
    • Find and select "Assessment".
  3. Select Therapeutic Recreation Assessment:
    • Locate and select the "Therapeutic Recreation Assessment" form.
  4. Fill Out the Form:
    • Complete all fields, including description, historical background, recreational involvement, data evaluation, and review.
  5. Save the Form:
    • Save the completed form.
    • A record will be created with an assigned ID.
  6. Additional Details:
    • Ensure accurate entry of all relevant information, including personal history, vital lab results, medications, personal team and encounters, prescriptions, goals, and progress.
  7. Review and Print:
    • Review the entered information.
    • Print the form if needed, displaying goals, related objectives, and teaching
