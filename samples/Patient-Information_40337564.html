DrCloudEHR : Patient Information

Created by Tejasvini Aurangabadkar, last modified by Kalyan Chakravartin on Sep 11, 2020

Patient Information fields types allows the user to auto-populate patients data in the field, information like name, DOB and the like from the patient's demographics into the form.

The following are the basic field types:

FieldsDescription
Facility Displays a list of facilities in the organization.
Provider / UserDisplays a list of users in the organization.
Full NameDisplays patients full name. 
PIDDisplays Patients unique ID.
Service DateDisplays the date service date of an encounter, date when the patient meets the provider/clinician.
AddressDisplays residentials address of the patient.
Admit FacilityDisplays the name of the facility where a patient was admitted.
Admit DateDisplays the date when the patient was admitted in the facility.
AgeDisplays the age of the patient in years and days.
DOBDisplays the date of birth of the patient.
Phone NumbersDisplays the patients home and mobile numbers.
S.S #Displays the patients social security number.
Add Patients ImageDisplays the patients display photograph.
Marital StatusDisplays the patients marital status.
GenderDisplays the patients gender (Male or female).
Telephone NumberDisplays the patients cell number.
Insurance Displays the patients primary insurance name.
VitalDisplays vitals information of the patient.
RaceDisplays patients race, region where the patient belong to.
Ethnicity Displays the patients culture/Nation.
Discharged Date Displays the date when the patient was discharged from the facility.
