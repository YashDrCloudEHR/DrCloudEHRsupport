DrCloudEHR : 13. Adding External Files to a Persons Chart

Created by Pankaj Bhagat on Aug 19, 2024
  1. Navigate to the person's chart. On the right side, locate the section labeled "Files."
  2. Click on "Add Files." This will allow you to upload files that have been shared with you recently.
  3. To upload external files, select "Upload Files." This action will open a window displaying the files on your computer.
  4. Choose the file you wish to upload. For example, if a doctor has sent back consent forms from a medical appointment, ensure that you have scanned and saved these documents on your computer.
  5. Once you select the file, it will be uploaded and added to the person's chart.

This process is particularly useful for uploading medical consent forms filled out by physicians after appointments.
