Incoming faxes 

After you enable the InterFax Feature, it becomes available at Practice tab.

  1. Click on the Practice tab.
  2. On the left navigation menu, click Administration > Fax Center. The Fax Center page opens.
  3. The Fax Center displays incoming and outgoing faxes. By default the incoming fax page is displayed.

The Incoming faxes

Add a New Fax

  1. Click on the Practice tab.
  2. On the left navigation menu,click Administration > Fax Center.The Fax Center page opens.
  3. The Fax Center displays incoming and outgoing faxes. By default the incoming fax page is displayed.
  4. Click on the Outgoing Fax tab.This displays the Outgoing faxes sent with the status as success/inprocess/failure.
  5. Click on Send Fax button.Send Fax page opens.
  6. Enter the following field values.
    • Fax number: Enter the recipient fax number.
    • Contact: Enter the recipient name.
    • Subject: Enter the fax subject.
    • Reply to email: Enter the senders email address for recipient info.
    • Attach File: Click Browse and select the file to send fax.
  7. Attach a file clicking on Choose File button.
  8. Click on Send button.
  9. The sent records can be edited,resend and reviewed from the outgoing fax tab.

Inbox

  1. Click on the Practice tab.
  2. On the left navigation menu, click Administration > Fax Center.The Fax Center page opens.
  3. The Fax Center displays incoming and outgoing faxes.By default the incoming fax page is displayed.
  4. The downloaded messages from interfax to the local database are displayed in the incoming tab.
  5. Click on Refresh to get new messages.
  6. Select the checkboxes and click on Mark as Read button,It turns the record to Bold and viceversa with Mark as UnRead Button.
  7. Select a patient and Click on Attach Button to attach a patient.
  8. Select a patient and Click on Detach Button to detach a patient.

The Inbox

Outgoing Faxes

  1. Click on the Practice tab.
  2. On the left navigation menu, click Administration > Fax Center. The Fax Center page opens.
  3. The Fax Center displays incoming and outgoing faxes. By default the incoming fax page is displayed.
  4. Click on the Outgoing Fax tab. This displays the Outgoing faxes sent with the status as success/inprocess/failure.
  5. Outgoing Faxes are displayed with values of Transaction ID, Fax Number, Contact, Subject, Reply Address, Send Date, Status, User Name, and Action.
  6. Preview, Resend, and Refresh icons are provided on the Action column against each outgoing fax.
    • Preview: to Preview the fax content in a separate window.
    • Re-send fax: To re-send the same fax to the same recipient.
    • Refresh fax status: To refresh the fax status.
