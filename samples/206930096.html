DrCloudEHR : 11. How to Enter Vitals in a Patient's Chart

Created by Pankaj Bhagat on Aug 19, 2024

Follow these instructions to accurately input vital signs into a patient's chart.

  1. Navigate to the patient's page and locate the vitals tab. If the tab is not visible, select the "more" option to find it.
  2. Once you have selected the vitals tab, click on the "new" button to begin entering the vitals.
  3. Input the patient's vital signs. The ideal body weight is based on your calculations of what you believe the person's body weight should be.
  4. Enter any relevant numbers you have for the patient. Note that circumference measurements may not be required in some organizations.
  5. You can add additional notes in the provided section. This may include the Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) or other optional items.
  6. Remember that the only required fields are the weight, height, and date.
  7. After filling in the necessary information, save the data. The patient's vitals will then be stored in their chart for future reference.
