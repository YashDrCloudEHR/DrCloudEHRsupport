DrCloudEHR : Managing Provider eSignatures

Created by Dan Hynes on Sep 22, 2020

You can use signature pads from the following companies with DrCloudEHR:

  • Scriptel
  • Topaz


  1. Click the Settings tab.
  2. Click the Preferences tab in the left navigation bar, and then click the Devices tab.
  3. Select the signature pad device you are using from the Digital Signature Pad menu.
  4. Click Save.
  5. Click the Manage eSignature tab in the left navigation bar.
