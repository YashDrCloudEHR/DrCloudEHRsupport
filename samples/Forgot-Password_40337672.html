DrCloudEHR : Forgot Password

Created by Tejasvini Aurangabadkar, last modified on Feb 26, 2018

If you forget your password after registration, click the Forgot Password link on the Login page.  The following pop-up window appears:

Enter either your Username or e-mail address, and click Submit. 

You receive a mail from The DrCloudEHR support team. Open the mail and follow the instructions to reset your password.
