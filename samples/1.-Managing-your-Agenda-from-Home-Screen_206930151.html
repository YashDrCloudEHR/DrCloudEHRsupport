DrCloudEHR : 1. Managing your Agenda from Home Screen

Created by Pankaj Bhagat on Aug 19, 2024

Managing your agenda effectively is crucial for staying organized. Hereâ€™s a concise guide on how to create and manage appointments directly from your homepage.

  1. View Your Agenda: Upon accessing your homepage, check your agenda for any scheduled meetings. For instance, a house ISP meeting may be listed, indicated by it not being assigned to a specific person.
  2. Create a New Event: Select the option to create a new event. This allows you to add another agenda item.
  3. Choose Event Type: You can schedule a clinical or service appointment related to a specific individual or opt for a generic event that does not pertain to any person.
  4. Open New Event Box: If you select a generic event, a new event box will appear. Here, you can input various details.
  5. Input Event Information: Fill in the necessary information, including the location and subject of the event. You can either type in the subject or select from a dropdown menu.
  6. Assign the Event: For example, if you want to schedule a weekly QIDP meeting, assign it to yourself if you will be managing it.
  7. Set Date and Time: Specify the start and end time for the event. For instance, you might schedule the meeting for Friday from 3 PM to 4 PM.
  8. Add Description: You have the option to include additional information in the description field.
  9. Save the Event: Once all necessary details are filled out, save the event to finalize it.

This process allows you to efficiently manage appointments and keep your agenda organized on your homepage.
