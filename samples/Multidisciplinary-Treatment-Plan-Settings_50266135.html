DrCloudEHR : Multidisciplinary Treatment Plan Settings

Created by Tejasvini Aurangabadkar on May 02, 2018

The Plan Settings option enables the user to associate Progress forms to the required facility in the Mutlidisciplinary Treatment Plan. Only the Administrative user can access the Plan Settings option. Users raise request when they want any form to be associated with their treatment plan. After the form is associated, it appears in the ADD Progress section.

To associate the Progress form to a facility, an Administrator must perform the following steps:

  1. Click the Plan Settings tab on the right corner in the Multidisciplinary Treatment Plan page.
    or 
    Navigate to Practice > Administrative > Multidisciplinary Treatment Plan.
    The following page appears:

  2. Select the required facility from the Facility drop down list. 
  3. Select the required progress forms and click Select. The forms appear in the Add Progress section of your plan.
    4. Note: If you have assigned a form to a specific facility, the form appears along with the default forms for the selected facility.  
  4. Basic Plan Settings: This section contains the following options:
    1. Plans, Objectives, Needs, and Goals are based on a patients existing diagnoses: When you select this option, the Condition List section appears in the Treatment plan.

    2. Include Weaknesses - Plans will include a section with a multiple selection of weaknesses to augment a treatment plan: When you select this option, the Weakness section appears in the Treatment plan.

    3. Include a readonly list of all ACTIVE patient diagnoses in plan interface and printouts: When you select this option, the Active Diagnosis section appears in the Treatment plan.

  5. Click Save.
