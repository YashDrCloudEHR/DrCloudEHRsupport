Company Policies and Guidelines

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. All remote workers must have reliable internet connection and maintain core hours of 10 AM to 3 PM EST for team collaboration. Remote work requests must be submitted at least one week in advance.

Vacation and Time Off
Vacation time accrues at a rate of 1.5 days per month for full-time employees. Employees can carry over up to 5 unused vacation days to the next year. All vacation requests must be approved by your direct manager at least 2 weeks in advance.

Dress Code
Business casual attire is required in the office. This includes collared shirts, slacks, or appropriate business skirts. Jeans are permitted on Fridays. Please avoid overly casual clothing such as t-shirts, shorts, or flip-flops.

Work Hours
Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval. Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM when all team members should be available.

Performance Reviews
Annual performance reviews are conducted in December. Employees will receive feedback on their goals, achievements, and areas for development. Performance reviews determine salary adjustments and promotion eligibility.
